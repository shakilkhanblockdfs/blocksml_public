# Assessing Candidates on Prompt Engineering Skills

_Last updated: Jan 20, 2025, 3:47 PM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

Prompt engineering is a valuable skill to assess in today's AI-driven world. These questions are a powerful tool for evaluating candidates' ability to craft effective prompts. Follow these steps to add these questions to a test:

**Step 1: Accessing the Question**

1.  **Navigate to the Library:**

    - Go to the **Library** and use the filter options to search for **Prompt Engineering**.

2.  **Select a Question:**

    - Select a prompt engineering question from the available list and click on the title to view the details, such as description, expected outputs, and evaluation criteria.

**Step 2: Adding the Question to a Test**

1.  **Create or Open a Test:**

    - Navigate to the **Tests** section and create a new test or open an existing one.

2.  **Add the Question:**

    - Click on **Add Questions** and search for the desired prompt engineering question.

    - Alternatively, click on the three dots icon and select **Add to Test**.

**Tip**: You can add or combine multiple questions with other types, such as coding or multiple-choice questions.

**Step 3: Reviewing Candidate Results**

1.  **Summary Report:** Once the test is completed, view the **Summary Report** to see the candidate's overall score, including their performance on prompt engineering questions.

2.  **Detailed Report:** For a deeper analysis, open the **Detailed Report**, where you can:

    - Play back the candidate's activity during the prompt engineering question.

    - Review the prompts they crafted and how they iterated on their solutions.
