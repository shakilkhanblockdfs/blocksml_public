# Upgrade Plan Upgrading plan

_Last updated: Mar 10, 2026, 12:19 PM (Last updated 2 months ago)_

You can upgrade your plan if your current subscription no longer meets your needs.

# Upgrading plan

To upgrade plan:

1.  Log in to **HackerRank for Work** using your credentials.

2.  Go to **Settings** from the profile drop-down.

3.  Select **Change Plan** in the **Subscription** section.

You can also upgrade your plan from the **Billing** page by clicking **View Other Plans** under **Current Plan**.
