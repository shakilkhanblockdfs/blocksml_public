# Email Notifications

_Last updated: Dec 26, 2024, 3:12 PM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

You can adjust email notification preferences for your HackerRank account by following these steps:

#### Accessing Email Notification Settings

1.  Click the **arrow** next to the user icon in the top-right corner of the home page.

2.  From the drop-down menu, select **Settings**, and then choose **Email Notifications** in the left navigation pane.

#### Configuring Test Notifications

- **Default Setting**: You will receive an email notification after a candidate completes a test.

- **Custom Options**:

  - Disable notifications for test completions.

  - Enable notifications only if a candidate's score exceeds the cutoff score.

  **Tip**: Specify the cutoff score in a test's **Advanced Setting**. For details, refer to the [Modifying Score Settings for Tests](#) article.

#### Configuring Interview Notifications

- Enable or disable the option to send an email when an interview ends automatically.\
  **Default**: Disabled.

- Enable or disable the option to send an email if you provide feedback indicating a poor interview experience.\
  **Default**: Enabled. This email helps collect additional details to improve your experience.

#### Configuring Product Update Notifications

- Enable or disable the option to receive product update emails.\
  **Default**: Enabled.

#### Finalizing Your Changes

- Click **Save** to apply your preferences.
