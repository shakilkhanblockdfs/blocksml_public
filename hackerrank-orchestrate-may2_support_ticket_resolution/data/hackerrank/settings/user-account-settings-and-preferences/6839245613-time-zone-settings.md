# Time Zone Settings

_Last updated: Dec 13, 2024, 5:06 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

## Overview

You can change your time zone settings to reflect the time zone in which you are located. By default, HackerRank selects the time zone based on your location while creating the account. This article will walk you through the steps in modifying the Time Zone settings.

## Modifying the Time Zone Settings

1.  Click on the arrow next to the user icon on the top right corner of the home page.

2.  Click on the **Settings** option from the drop-down menu that appears.
