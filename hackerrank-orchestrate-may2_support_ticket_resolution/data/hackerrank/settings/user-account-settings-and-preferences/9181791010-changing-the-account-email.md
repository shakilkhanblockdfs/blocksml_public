# Changing the Account Email

_Last updated: Dec 13, 2024, 5:06 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

It is not possible for a user to change their account email. However, you can email support@hackerrank.com from the account that you want to change to request the primary email change. Once we verify that you have access to the email account, we can change the email address for you.
