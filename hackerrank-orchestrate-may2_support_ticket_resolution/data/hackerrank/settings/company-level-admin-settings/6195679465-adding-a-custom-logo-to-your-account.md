# Update Company Logo Prerequisite Updating the company logo

_Last updated: Dec 15, 2025, 3:30 PM (Last updated 4 months ago)_

You can update the company logo that appears in candidate invitation emails and across your HackerRank for Work account.

# Prerequisite

You must have Company Admin access.

# Updating the company logo

To update the logo for your company account:

1.  Log in to **HackerRank for Work** using your credentials.

2.  Select your profile icon in the upper-right corner.

3.  Select **Settings** from the drop-down menu.

4.  In the left panel, select **Company Settings** under the **Company** section.

5.  In the **Company Logo** section, select **Change.**

6.  Upload a new logo in **SVG or PNG** format (maximum size: **800 KB**).

7.  Review the preview and confirm your changes. Your new company logo updates immediately.

**Note:** If the user who sends the invitation belongs to a single team and that team has a logo set, HackerRank uses the team logo for all invitations. For more information, see [📄 Set Team Logo and Sender Name](/articles/5319929416).
