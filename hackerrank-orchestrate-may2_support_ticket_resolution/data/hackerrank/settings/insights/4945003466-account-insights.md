# Account Insights

_Last updated: Dec 13, 2024, 5:14 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

The Insights tab on the HackerRank platform provides statistical dashboards from your company's utilization of the HackerRank account by your recruiting and technical staff. As a company admin, you can access the Insights page to gain an analytical knowledge of how your hiring teams are using HackerRank in promoting your organization to hire the best talents while being able to save revenues. These data visualization tools and dashboards allow you to drill down into your data while maintaining a secure, birds-eye view of operations.

You can also download Candidate performance reports and reports specific to utilizing your HackerRank company account.

## Accessing Insights

Prerequisites

- You must log in as a Company Admin user

On the Home page, click on the Insights tab to view the various analysis in the following categorical sections:

- Test Usage

- Interview Usage

- Interview Questions

## Additional Features

Here are some of the key features that you can use to make the most use of the dashboards. These features let you modify a report to your requirements to see more detail and drill down to gain deeper insights into your reports.

There are additional features that let you schedule and automate the distribution of reports, insights, data sets, and query results. 

### Data Download

You can download the entire data for dashboards, or an option is available to download each of the reports that are available inside these dashboards.  Data can be downloaded in the form of PDFs or CSVs.

### Download Test Data

To download the complete dashboard information, *c*lick on the Download Test Data button under each of these dashboards. You can select a date range from the available options or set a custom date range when you click on the Download button.

### Drill-Down Capabilities

Click on the numbers in any of the widgets  to drill down and check the detailed information. For example, click on the count/number in the Total Test Invites widget from the Test Usage dashboard. This would open a dialog where you can see detailed data for the widget. This dialog also gives you the option to download displayed data by clicking the "download" option on the top right corner. Data would be downloaded in ".csv" format in your computer.

### Filtering 

You can limit the data you see on your dashboard by adding filters. Use the filter icon to expand the filter section and select the desired date range.
