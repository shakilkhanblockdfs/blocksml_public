# Update Team Settings Prerequisite Updating team settings

_Last updated: Mar 3, 2026, 3:27 PM (Last updated 2 months ago)_

HackerRank allows you to modify team settings for an existing team, such as the team name, region, and department.

# Prerequisite

You must have Company Admin or Team Admin access.

# Updating team settings

To update team settings:

1.  Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.

2.  Select your profile icon in the upper-right corner.

3.  Select **Teams Management** from the drop-down menu.

4.  Go to the **Teams** tab.
