# Modify Entitlements for Interviewers Prerequisite Modifying entitlements for interviewers Available interviewer entitlements

_Last updated: Mar 12, 2026, 2:43 PM (Last updated 1 month ago)_

You can modify interviewer entitlements to control access to tests, interviews, questions, and candidates.

# Prerequisite

You must have created a team.

# Modifying entitlements for interviewers

To modify entitlements for interviewers in a team:

1.  Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.

2.  Select your profile icon in the upper-right corner.

3.  Select **Teams Management** from the drop-down menu.

    ![team manage.png](https://assets.usepylon.com/e6a58e21-be80-4777-9eaf-f73beeee94d9%2F1773208948896-teammanage.png?Expires=253370764800&Signature=kxcmobInAPAfzZvgI~7U9sX8xf~02aOTjop5JQHUny1ruVBKLokYjmlLYSaCSSMZjdho9C0Zs5v0MmT0WWGOwi0GiOenbioIJH3L62dPvZtr6i1PWhRBXUn7NJCWSHsF2Y9Yo7P1ZxcoS2N~tMlJz6Y3Hk~Vtc0cMAs2EHOv5FUcqrPTMS3fK-ubIsv747-a-Oh2oiCpkuIQ9e8~uIbN3qpigIkzOeqgCTnUa5zyYH7DcXawbO9Gi2VrRqdE6iLOWmHqZk8xfo0tY8EuFVmzsztLvX~hG~kFEpY7ayUL5UYfu8aR3vWQKwJ~uKJNcrUNggX8oPf9kWWE7ZUVfnPXYQ__&Key-Pair-Id=K3NV4LZ47N8M46)

# Available interviewer entitlements

You can manage the following entitlement categories for interviewers:

- Tests

- Interviews

- Questions

- Interview templates

- Interview scorecards

- Exports
