# Team Member Status

_Last updated: Dec 27, 2024, 11:15 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

HackerRank for Work allows you to categorize team members based on their current status, such as **Activated**, **Not Activated**, or **Locked**. A team member’s status indicates their position within the organization. You can search for team members by status, review their status, and take relevant actions as needed.

### Steps to Identify Team Member Status

1.  **Access Teams Management**

    - Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account.

    - Click the **arrow** next to the user icon in the top-right corner of the homepage.

    - Select **Teams Management** from the dropdown menu.

2.  **Navigate to the Users Tab**

    - On the **Teams Management** page, click the **Users** tab.

    - The page displays a list of all users and details such as team, region, and department.

3.  **Filter by Status**

    - Use the **Status** filter at the top of the page to refine the user list.

    - Select one of the following options from the dropdown:

      - **Activated**: Indicates that the user has activated their account using the activation email and is part of one or more teams.

      - **Not Activated**: Indicates that the user belongs to one or more teams but has not yet activated their account.

      - **Locked**: Indicates that the user is not part of any team and has no resources allocated.

4.  **View Users by Status**

    - Selecting any status option displays a list of users categorized under the chosen status.
