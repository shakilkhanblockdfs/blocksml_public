# Manage Team Members Prerequisite Managing team members

_Last updated: Mar 3, 2026, 3:30 PM (Last updated 2 months ago)_

HackerRank allows you to manage team members to control access and permissions. You can add or remove team members and update team member roles.

# Prerequisite

You must have Company Admin or Team Admin access.

# Managing team members

To manage team members:

1.  Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.

2.  Select your profile icon in the upper-right corner.

3.  Select **Teams Management** from the drop-down menu.

4.  Go to the **Teams** tab.

    ![teams.png](https://assets.usepylon.com/e6a58e21-be80-4777-9eaf-f73beeee94d9%2F7b0c9788-c811-4d97-987d-7e607f46ca89-1772517693085-teams.png-70dc7657-9b94-48de-8e7f-24e0015183e4?Expires=253370764800&Signature=DkvZsAGtgjZaTkjcFaKFW57odm16QTRy06Y0AAU0kb~6s41NgKqLkhojQzdyPTZSetJJ-s4W0TI1KT5l5P3SZO7DaOfciGf6D-zjxtT6QZ0LgUxijyEeUTNpIA7zdh6jobsWG5fhMlBjC5mCxS4~GRQu5izwm5SNphXjgM874ejIRbqLEDNwV4NiePSdBjVOjGnf9pcZbHnfP9sdvS~ijy~vbRI~zCsLPi64L68bDclRH6Fz41p~sDZKrDsgAavp6iG5jFi1LyZl7fPq~5~6f5No8jUq1g-3YQEgT57n6U7lAkJMnVOpx0EkdmfNLfNcTKfY~xrQtQ4PPzrIcZJgCw__&Key-Pair-Id=K3NV4LZ47N8M46)

5.  Select the team you want to update.

6.  Select the **Users** tab to add team members, update their roles, or remove team members.

## Adding an existing user to a team

To add an existing user to a team:

1.  Type or select users from the **Add Users** drop-down menu

2.  Click **Add**.

## Updating team member role

To modify the role of a team member:

1.  Locate the user in the list.

2.  Select the **Team Role** dropdown.

3.  Choose **Team Admin** or **Member**.

    ![team role update.png](https://assets.usepylon.com/e6a58e21-be80-4777-9eaf-f73beeee94d9%2F11800322-647f-4227-99a3-821f943cddf5-1772518239711-teamroleupdate.png-c990113b-fa2b-4398-aa7b-aa7367565fc3?Expires=253370764800&Signature=bmfzFBUD4vmgsXFMQZwEs~m6SdB8Wj2cxjL15PEpIUKwgnS~r~OrVOHntgC7dZ5CrEF28PSj3hLQ3uBk9UY-4YYtpJ~NNW19jrVq87RJNYC6JuAandPgKQZZO3dfkovcJehVHS0iSohVVJFCAdGyXyg5DBbZSjTRMQctpv0H7jpPC1fmNRneCoVVaI~74WmOiVMusQhdE0H6qFtDAMGkJ~jeawBnGvfXxDAGbYDs2ShRKA7wMs8UaKWuvFvBiwg4W0g18mNkTgYgstyuUfDWU16RJoVZzc6bBE03XAMbVaPJx5CYey4Ggw4dne8KTppetjUECrlxZJv19485qp7lRQ__&Key-Pair-Id=K3NV4LZ47N8M46)

## Removing a team member

To remove a team member from the team:

1.  Locate the user in the list.

2.  Select the **delete** icon in the **Action** column.
