# Create a Team Prerequisite Creating a team

_Last updated: Feb 23, 2026, 12:38 PM (Last updated 2 months ago)_

You can create teams in HackerRank to match your company structure. Members of a team can view all tests, interviews, and other resources created within that team.

# Prerequisite

You must have Company Admin access.

# Creating a team

To create a team:

1.  Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.

2.  Select your profile icon in the upper-right corner.

3.  Select **Teams Management** from the drop-down menu.

    - **Team Name:** Name of the team you want to create.

    - (Optional) **Region**: Indicates where the team operates, such as a city, country, or geographic area (for example, APAC or EMEA).

    - (Optional) **Department**: Indicates the company division the team belongs to, such as Sales, Engineering, or Human Resources.

    - (Optional) **Add users**: Type or select the users from the drop-down menu.

      **Note:** The **Add users** drop-down list displays only existing users. To add a new user, see [📄 Invite a New User](/articles/9482219268).

7.  Click **Add**. 

HackerRank creates the team and displays it under **Team Management** \> **Teams**.

Each team can have one or more **Team Admins**. For more information, see [📄 Grant Team Admin Access](/articles/9611303302).

**Note:** To update team details, see [📄 Updating a Team](/articles/6568033805).
