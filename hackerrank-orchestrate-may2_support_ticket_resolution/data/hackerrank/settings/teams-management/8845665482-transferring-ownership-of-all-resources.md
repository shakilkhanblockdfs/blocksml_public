# Transfer Resource Ownership Prerequisite Transferring resource ownership

_Last updated: Mar 12, 2026, 2:44 PM (Last updated 1 month ago)_

HackerRank allows you to transfer ownership of all resources, such as tests and questions, from one user to another. Transferring ownership ensures that no critical content is lost during user transitions.

# Prerequisite

You must have Company Admin access.

# Transferring resource ownership

To transfer ownership of all resources:

1.  Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.

2.  Select your profile icon in the upper-right corner.

3.  Select **Teams Management** from the drop-down menu.
