# Searching for a Team

_Last updated: Dec 13, 2024, 5:11 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

## Overview

HackerRank for Work enables you to search for a particular team for easy access to shared resources in the team. This article will walk you through the steps involved in searching for a team. 

## Steps to Search a Team

**Prerequisites**

- You must be logged in to your HackerRank for Work account.

**Steps**

1.  On successfully logging into your HackerRank for Work account, click on the arrow next to the user icon on the top right corner of the homepage. This leads you to the **Teams Management** page.

3.  On the search bar, type the name of the team you are searching for.

4.  The team(s) that match the searched word(s) will appear on the right pane.
