# Delete an Interview Prerequisites Deleting an Interview

_Last updated: Sep 29, 2025, 12:28 AM (Last updated 7 months ago)_

You can delete a scheduled HackerRank Interview at any time. When you delete an interview, HackerRank automatically sends email notifications to the candidate and all interviewers.

# Prerequisites

You must have already created and scheduled an interview.

# Deleting an Interview

To delete a scheduled interview:

1.  Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials. 

2.  Go to the **Interviews** \> **Interview List**. The page displays a list of all interviews you have created. 

3.  Click the more options menu (**⋮**) next to the interview you want to delete.

4.  Click **Delete Interview**.
