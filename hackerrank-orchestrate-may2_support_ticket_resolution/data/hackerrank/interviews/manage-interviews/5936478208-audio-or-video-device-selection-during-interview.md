# Audio or Video Device Selection During Interview

_Last updated: Dec 15, 2024, 10:54 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

## Selecting Audio or Video Device During an Interview

#### Prerequisites

- The interviewer and the candidate must ensure that browser or system permissions are granted to use the microphone and/or webcam during the interview.

#### Steps

- When you start a call during the interview, a pop-up window will appear, prompting you to select devices for your webcam, microphone, and speakers.

- A drop-down menu will list the available devices on your computer. From the available devices, select the preferred webcam, microphone, or speakers.
