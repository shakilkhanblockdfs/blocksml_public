# Export Interview List Prerequisite Exporting interview list

_Last updated: Apr 22, 2026, 10:17 AM (Last updated 4 days ago)_

You can download the interview list as a `.csv` file to view, share, or analyze interview data offline.

# Prerequisite

You have created at least one interview. For more information, see [📄 Create an Interview](/articles/8404005945).

# Exporting interview list

To export the interview list:

1.  Log in to your HackerRank for Work account using your credentials.

2.  Go to **Interviews** \> **Interview List**.

3.  (Optional) Use the search bar or filters to find specific interviews. For more information, see [Search and filter interviews](https://support.hackerrank.com/articles/8404005945-creating-an-interview#search-and-filter-interviews).

4.  Click the export icon.
