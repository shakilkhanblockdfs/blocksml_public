# HackerRank SkillUp

_Last updated: Dec 13, 2024, 4:58 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

### Introduction to SkillUp

SkillUp is a solution to support internal upskilling and mobility within your organization. It allows individuals to assess their skill proficiency and receive personalized learning plans for developing skills relevant to their current or future roles. 

Skill development is validated through badges and role certifications, ensuring employees and organizations can track growth. For employers, SkillUp offers a comprehensive view of the skill inventory within the organization, aiding in workforce planning by identifying strengths and skill gaps.

### SkillUp Use Cases

SkillUp creates value for employers and employees by providing visibility into your workforce's current and future state, enabling data-driven development.

### For Employers

- **Workforce Planning**: Gain insight into your organization’s skills distribution. Use this data to understand workforce strengths and gaps, supporting effective upskilling initiatives.

- **Talent Certification**: Accurately certify current skill levels, eliminating assumptions and guesswork. Use this data to make informed decisions about workforce development.

- **Learning and Development Tracking**: Monitor the progress and effectiveness of learning programs using comprehensive reporting tools to ensure continuous improvement.

- **Internal Mobility**: Identify potential Employees for internal job roles by reviewing verified skill profiles and progress in development. This feature can support career advancement and reduce employee turnover.

### For Developers

#### **Upskilling Resources**

- **Vast Learning Library:** Access a comprehensive library of learning materials, including courses, tutorials, and hands-on projects. These resources are tailored to enhance your skills across various roles and technologies, helping you upskill and benchmark your proficiency.

- **Verified Skill Profiles:** Create and maintain a verified skill profile on **SkillUp**. This profile highlights your proficiencies, achievements, and technical expertise, giving a comprehensive overview of your skills.

- **Curated Learning Paths: SkillUp provides customized learning paths b**ased on your current skill level, career goals, and organizational needs. These pathways ensure efficient, targeted skill development aligned with your growth and organizational objectives.

By leveraging **SkillUp**, developers can take charge of their skill development, earn credentials, and explore internal mobility opportunities. Employers can make informed decisions about technical talent, drive upskilling initiatives, and ensure a skilled, productive workforce aligned with business objectives.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How is a proficiency level determined?**

Skill Ratings determine the proficiency level. HackerRank determines the cut-offs for each proficiency in a skill-based on inputs from SMEs and how the top 10% of the employees have scored in a particular assessment that determines the skill proficiency.

In the case of custom assessments (tailored to meet the organization's requirements), the organizations set the cut-offs.

**What if I take multiple assessments that assess the same skill?**

The Skill Rating would be based on the results from the most recent assessment taken. This will help us keep the Skill Rating accurate and up-to-date to enable your decision-making process.

**How can I access past Skill Ratings?**

In the profile of an employee, you can go to the Skill Details section to see the progress of Skill Ratings over a period of time.

**How many retakes are allowed for an assessment?**

The employer will determine the number of retakes for each assessment based on their policies.

The cool-down period is determined as the days to pass before taking the next retake.  

**What is the difference between Skill Ratings and Badges?**

Skill Rating is a quantitative indicator of an individual's proficiency in technical skills.

Badges, on the other hand, are a way of acknowledging an achievement or a skill acquisition at a skill proficiency level. You can receive a badge when you have taken an assessment and reached proficiency level.

For example, If you take a Java assessment and achieve the required Skill Rating for expert proficiency, you will receive the Java (Expert) ^ badge.

**What happens to my skill rating if the assessment is retired or updated?**

If an assessment is deleted from HRW, it is considered retired. You will continue to see the assessment scores in the Assessment History, but your Skill Rating will reflect the most recent Rating before the retired assessment.

If the content of an assessment changes, it is considered updated. However, your skill rating will not be affected if an assessment is updated. 

**How can I add custom learning resources to SkillUp?**

You must provide an Excel sheet with the links to custom learning resources like Pluralsight videos or Udacity courses. Within two business days, the HackerRank team will manually add these links to the relevant skills. In the future, you can add custom resources through the admin dashboard. 

**Can I influence the content in certification assessments? **

No, you cannot influence or customize the content in the certification assessments. Hackerrank is taking a strategic bet on AI-first learning and will invest in providing guided, immersive AI tutors for each skill and role. The certification content will be Hackerrank's proprietary AI-powered assessments.

**What is the difference between a badge and a certification? **

A badge is earned for demonstrating proficiency in a skill after passing a skill assessment. Certification is earned for a role that combines multiple skills after passing a comprehensive role assessment that includes prerequisites of earning the relevant skill badges. 

**Can I transfer skill badges earned outside the company (e.g., on the HackerRank community) to my employer's SkillUp account?**

You cannot transfer external skill badges into your organization's SkillUp account. SkillUp treats personal community certifications separately from professional employer certifications to maintain data privacy and boundaries.

**How often are the global in-demand skills updated on the SkillUp platform?**

The global in-demand skills showcased on SkillUp are updated annually based on HackerRank's skills report research. However, the company is exploring increasing the frequency of these updates.
