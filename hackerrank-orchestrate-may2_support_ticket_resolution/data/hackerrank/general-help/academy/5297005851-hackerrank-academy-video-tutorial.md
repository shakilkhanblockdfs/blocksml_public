# HackerRank Academy

_Last updated: Dec 21, 2024, 12:35 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

The HackerRank Academy is a comprehensive learning platform and your go-to resource for mastering the HackerRank platform. Our academy offers bite-sized video courses to help you navigate and optimize your use of HackerRank. Learn best practices, get expert tips, and access additional materials to streamline your hiring process.

## Accessing HackerRank Academy

**1. Through the** [HackerRank Academy URL](https://hackerrank.northpass.com/app)

- Provide your name and work email address to log in

**2.** **Through the HackerRank Platform** 

- Log in to your [HackerRank for Work](https://www.hackerrank.com/work/login) account

- Scroll down and click on **Academy** at the bottom of the page to log in and start learning
