# How to Move Questions Across Sections in a Test?

_Last updated: Dec 13, 2024, 5:08 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

You can organize the Question in your test under logical categories called sections. You can create multiple sections within a Test and use the **Move** option to organize the Questions in different sections.

1.  Click **Tests** on the home page, select the required Test.

2.  In the Questions tab, add Questions to the Test from the Library.

3.  To create a section, click the plus sign and select **Create section**. 

**Section 1** is added, and by default, all the Test Questions now belong to this section.

*Questions added to Section 1*

1.  Click **Add another section** to add additional sections.

2.  Select Question(s) from **Section 1** and click **Move**. From the list, select the alternate section to move the Question.

*Question moved to another section*

1.  In a similar manner, select the required Questions from any section and click **Move** to move them to an alternate section.

2.  In a scenario where the Test Questions are not added to any section, they are listed under the "**Unassigned Questions**" category. You can select the Questions from this list and click **Move** to move them to the relevant Sections.

*Moving Questions from the unassigned category*

**Note**: It is recommended that you use the **Move** option and avoid the 'drag-and-drop method to move Questions from one section to another.
