# How to Rename a Test

_Last updated: Dec 23, 2024, 3:25 PM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

You can rename an existing test by clicking on the pen icon beside the test name.

Once you have completed changing the test name, you can save it by clicking on the green tick icon. Alternatively, you can choose not to save it by clicking on the close icon.

You can also change the name in the **Settings** tab, under **General.**

**Note**: We recommend you not change the name of an ongoing Test.
