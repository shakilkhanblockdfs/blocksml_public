# How can I delete a Question that I No Longer Require?

_Last updated: Dec 13, 2024, 5:09 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

You cannot delete the questions in HackerRank Library. However, you can archive a question and restore it at a later time as required. Read about the steps to archive a test or question [here](https://support.hackerrank.com/hc/en-us/articles/115006379968-Archiving-a-test).
