# Grant Test Access Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

_Last updated: Sep 1, 2025, 12:26 PM (Last updated 8 months ago)_

HackerRank for Work allows admins to grant access to a test for specific users or teams.

## Prerequisites

You must be the test owner or have **Editor** access.

## Granting test access

To grant test access to users or teams:

1.  Log in to your HackerRank for Work account using your credentials.

2.  Go to the **Tests** tab.

3.  Open the test you want to share.

4.  Click **Share.**

5.  In the **Share Test** dialog box, enter a user or team name in the **Search users and teams** field.

6.  Choose a sharing role from the drop-down list:

    - **Viewer**: Can view the test and send invites.

    - **Evaluator**: Can view the test, send invites, and review candidates.

    - **Editor**: Can view and edit the test, send invites, review candidates, and share the test.

7.  Click **Save**.

After you save the changes, the users or teams and their assigned roles appear below the search field.

**Note:** Any teams the test owner is a part of automatically have access to the test.

# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Can I disable automated team-based sharing?**

No. You cannot disable automated team-based sharing.

**Who can share a test?**

The following users can share a test:

- The test owner

- Company administrators

- Users or teams with **Editor** access

**What does transferring ownership do?**

When you transfer test ownership to another user:

- You become an **Editor.**

- The new owner gains full control of the test.

- Team-based sharing continues to determine which teams have default access. You cannot remove access from teams that have access by default.
