_Last updated: Jan 23, 2025, 3:26 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

## Overview

## Reaching Out for the Integration Help

To know more about how to integrate the Goodtime ATS with HackerRank, you can reach out to the HackerRank point of contact or raise a support request by writing to [support@hackerrank.com](mailto:support@hackerrank.com).

You can also refer to the [Goodtime website](https://goodtime.io/fan-love/amazing-integration-with-our-ats-and-hackerrank/).
