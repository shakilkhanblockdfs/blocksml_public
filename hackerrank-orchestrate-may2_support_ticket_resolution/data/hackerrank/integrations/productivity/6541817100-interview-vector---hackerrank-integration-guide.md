# Interview Vector - HackerRank Integration Guide

_Last updated: Dec 24, 2024, 6:37 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

## Overview

HackerRank integrates with Interview Vector to facilitate a seamless screening experience for users.

You can include HackerRank tests as part of your assessment process through Interview Vector. Users can send HackerRank Test invites and review test scores and reports within Interview Vector. Test result report shows up inside the candidate's Interview Vector profile.

## Reaching Out for the Integration Help

To know more about how to integrate Interview Vector with HackerRank, you can reach out to the HackerRank point of contact or raise a support request by writing to [support@hackerrank.com](mailto:support@hackerrank.com).

You can refer to the [Interview Vector website](https://interviewvector.com/).
