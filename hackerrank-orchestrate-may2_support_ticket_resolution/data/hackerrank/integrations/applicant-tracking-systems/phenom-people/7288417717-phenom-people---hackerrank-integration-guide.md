# Phenom People - HackerRank Integration Guide

_Last updated: Dec 27, 2024, 6:10 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

## Overview

HackerRank integrates with Phenom for screening Tests. The integration between HackerRank and Phenom allows hiring teams to send HackerRank assessments and view candidate scores, directly through the Phenom platform. This reduces friction by eliminating the need to switch between multiple platforms.

## Reaching Out for the Integration Help

To know more about how to integrate the Phenom People ATS with HackerRank, you can reach out to the HackerRank point of contact or raise a support request by writing to [support@hackerrank.com](mailto:support@hackerrank.com).

For information about the Phenom People ATS, see the [Phenom People website](https://www.phenom.com/).
