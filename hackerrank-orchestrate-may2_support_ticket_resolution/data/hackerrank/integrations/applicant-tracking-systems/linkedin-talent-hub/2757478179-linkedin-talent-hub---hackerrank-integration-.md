# LinkedIn Talent Hub - HackerRank Integration

_Last updated: Dec 27, 2024, 6:31 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

## Overview

HackerRank integrates with LinkedIn for Tests and Interviews to facilitate a seamless interviewing experience to schedule remote Interviews from LinkedIn. Users can also send HackerRank Test invites to the candidates. The Test results and Interview feedback show up directly inside the candidate's LinkedIn profile.

LinkedIn Talent Hub users will be able to schedule and start technical video interviews directly from Talent Hub. This results in an efficient interviewing experience for the hiring team and the candidate.
