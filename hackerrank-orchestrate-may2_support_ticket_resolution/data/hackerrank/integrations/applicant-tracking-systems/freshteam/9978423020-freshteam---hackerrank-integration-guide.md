# Freshteam - HackerRank Integration Guide

_Last updated: Dec 27, 2024, 6:34 AM (Last updated 1 year ago)_

## Overview

HackerRank integrates with Freshteam for screening Tests. The integration between HackerRank and Freshteam allows hiring teams to send HackerRank assessments and view candidate scores, directly through the Freshteam platform. This reduces friction by eliminating the need to switch between multiple platforms, thus saving time, eliminating cognitive bias, and bringing more order to your recruiting.

To know more about how to integrate the Freshteam ATS with HackerRank, you can reach out to the HackerRank point of contact or raise a support request by writing to [support@hackerrank.com](mailto:support@hackerrank.com).

You can also refer to the [Freshteam ATS support articles](https://support.freshteam.com/support/solutions/articles/19000072352-integrating-hackerrank-with-freshteam).
